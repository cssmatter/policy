Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to 52-Week Bible Study For All. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure experience while you study and grow.

2. Data Collection & Usage

Our application collects certain information to provide a personalized experience:

  • Account Information: If you choose to sign in using Apple or Google, we collect your name, email address, and authentication tokens to sync your data across devices.
  • App Data & Progress: We securely store your reading progress, streaks, saved verses, and personal journal entries in our encrypted cloud database (Supabase) so you can access them anytime.
  • Guest Mode: If you use the app as a Guest, your progress is tracked anonymously.

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to ensure the app functions smoothly, handles purchases, and displays non-personalized advertisements:

  • Supabase (Database & Authentication)
  • Google AdMob (Non-personalized Advertising & Diagnostics)
  • RevenueCat (In-App Purchase Management)
  • Google Sign-In & Apple Sign-In (Authentication)

4. Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can delete your account and all associated data permanently at any time directly from the Profile/Settings screen within the app.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in our services or legal requirements. You will be notified of any significant changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or your data, please contact our support via the relevant app store feedback channel.
